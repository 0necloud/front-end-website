Enhance Productivity

In manufacturing processes, AI can be used to compare images of products to check if they are up to standard. Did you know that Domino's Pizza Inc. is using Artificial Intelligence to ensure that their pizza is perfect? The DOM Pizza Checker scans each pizza against a dataset that shows what a perfect pizza looks like. This has helped to ensure that their customers are happy.

AI has many other uses, such as in Research And Development, where AI searches for what customers prefer. Coca Cola's decision to release Cherry Sprite as a new flavour was based on monitoring data collected from the latest generation of self-service soft drinks fountains, which allow customers to mix their own drinks.
