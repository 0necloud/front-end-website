CYTEX was founded in 2003 with a mission in mind: to spread the knowledge of machine learning to others. We are partnered with many corporations, such as the Smart Nation and Digital Government Office (SNDGO), the Economic Development Board (EDB), the Infocomm Media Development Authority (IMDA). Annually, we host many events for the public to take part in. These include our Applied AI and Analytics Training and Consultancy programme, talks at Secondary Schools, as well as our showcases at the Singapore Expo during the SITEX exhibition. CYTEX has been awarded many achievements over the years, for our fine service to the public, as well as our innovative use of Artificial Intelligence technologies.
