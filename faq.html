FAQ

I do not have relevant experiences, can I still join?
Of course! We will be covering the basics of AI and teaching participants on the fundamentals of analysing data, so everyone is welcomed to join!

Why should I choose our company over other companies?
We have a rich history in the field of Artificial Intelligence, and our team is made up of experienced personnel from relevant fields. Our company has won several awards, and this training is sponsored by the Cyber Security Agency of Singapore.

Is there interaction with other participants?
Yes, there will be a team project, where each team has to come up with ideas and present them. You are allowed to form your own team or choose to be placed in other teams.

What if I am unable to make it on one of the days?
Unfortunately, we will not refund any money in case of absence, however, we will provide all participants with powerpoint slides containing the information covered, so you can review them anytime at your own time and pace. Also, this training programme takes place annually, so if you are unable to make it this year, there is always next year!

Is there food provided?
Yes, there will be snacks given out during short breaks between each session.

Can I attend both the training and consultation?
Yes! We will arrange time for you at the end of one of the training sessions for consultation.

Which room will the programme be held in?
There will be signs at the building's receptionist and personnel to guide participants to the venue.

Can I cancel my registration?
If you have to cancel your registration, you may do so until 30th November. Your money will be fully refunded and your details will be deleted from our system.

If you have any more questions, please contact us at support@cytex.com.sg
